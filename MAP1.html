WASTE MANAGEMENT

Waste Management or Waste Disposal are all the activities and actions required to manage waste from its inception to its final disposal. This includes amongst other things collection, transport, treatment, and disposal of waste together with monitoring and regulation. It also encompasses the legal and regulatory framework that relates to waste management encompasing guidance on recycling.

3R's

The three R's - reduce, reuse & recycle- all help to cut down on the amount of waste we throw away. They conserve natural resources, landfill space and energy. Plus, the 3 R's save land & money communities must use to dispose of waste in landfills.
